Chapter 1: What is Digital Literacy?

Understanding Digital Literacy

Digital literacy is the ability to find, evaluate, use, and create digital content effectively and safely. It includes understanding how technology works, how to use devices and software, and how to stay safe online.

üåü Why Digital Literacy Matters
  • Education: Access online learning and resources
  • Employment: Most jobs require basic digital skills
  • Communication: Stay connected with family and friends
  • Finance: Manage money through online banking
  • Information: Access news and knowledge instantly
  • Empowerment: Participate fully in digital society
