Our Mission

In an increasingly connected world, digital literacy and cybersecurity have become fundamental life skills. Code Geass is dedicated to bridging the digital divide in Africa by providing accessible, comprehensive, and culturally relevant cybersecurity education and protection tools.

We believe that every African internet user deserves to navigate the digital space with confidence, knowledge, and security. Through our three-pillar approach of education, verification, and protection, we're building a safer digital ecosystem for all.
