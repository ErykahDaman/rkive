Chapter 1: What is Digital Etiquette?

Understanding Digital Etiquette

Digital etiquette (or "netiquette") refers to the responsible and respectful way of communicating and behaving when using digital technology, including social media, email, messaging apps, and online forums.

Why Digital Etiquette Matters

üåç Your Digital Footprint

Everything you post, share, or comment on creates a permanent digital footprint. Future employers, schools, and others can see what you've shared online.

ü§ù Building Relationships

Good digital etiquette helps you maintain positive relationships with friends, family, colleagues, and your online community.

üõ°Ô∏è Protecting Yourself

Understanding digital etiquette helps you avoid cyberbullying, harassment, and other online threats.

üíº Professional Reputation

Your online behavior reflects on your character. Good digital etiquette can open doors to opportunities, while poor behavior can close them.

The Golden Rule Online

Treat others online the way you would want to be treated in person. Behind every screen is a real person with real feelings.
