Understanding Security Levels

🛡️ Safe

Website appears legitimate with no detected threats. Safe to proceed with normal caution.

⚠️ Caution

Some suspicious elements detected. Proceed with extra caution and verify information independently.

🚨 Danger

High risk detected. Known scam indicators present. Do not proceed or share personal information.

❓ Unknown

Insufficient data for assessment. Use additional verification methods before proceeding.
